Summary

Designer and engineer with ten years shipping products for technical users across data, infrastructure, healthcare, and mobility. Trained as an artist and self-taught in code, I work across the full stack of design — from early research and systems thinking through to production front-end. My strongest work lives at the intersection of complex data and the people who need to act on it: making dense, high-stakes information legible without flattening what makes it hard. I gravitate toward roles where design ownership extends into implementation, and where the gap between a great design and a shipped product is a problem I'm expected to close.
